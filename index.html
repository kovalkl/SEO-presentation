Semantic Markup

  • header - represents the header or top section of a webpage.
  • nav - indicates a navigation menu.
  • main - contains the primary content of a webpage.
  • section - defines a logically related group of content, such as a chapter in a book or a section of news articles.
  • aside - contains content that is tangentially related to the main content, often used for sidebars or additional information.
  • footer - represents the footer or bottom section of a webpage.
